ITBI

by admin — last modified Nov 26, 2013 01:36 PM — History

Feedback is the breakfast of champions is often told. In order to provide timely relevant feedback to everyone the data collection and presentation of the performance metrics must be automated so everyone can see at all times the current performance levels.

- Collect and aggregate data for better reporting and information presentation
- Allow ad-hoc and predefined reporting
- Distribute information realtime or scheduled
